How to Use This Page

Follow these steps to use the Maxima, Minima, and Related Rates Solver:

  • Maxima and Minima:
    • Enter a mathematical function in the "Enter a function" input box.
    • Click the "Solve Maxima and Minima" button.
    • The results will display critical points, maxima, minima, and detailed solution steps.
  • Related Rates:
    • Enter a time-dependent equation in the "Enter an equation with time dependency" input box.
    • Click the "Solve Related Rates" button.
    • The results will display the rate of change solution.
